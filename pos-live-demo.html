Submit a question. When configured, GPT-5.6 answers through the OpenAI API; otherwise the demo switches to a clearly labeled offline mode for manual answers. Proof-of-Silence™ evaluates the answer evidence and risk, signs the record, and adds it to a SHA-256 hash chain.

Transparent verdict logic

REJECTCritical action below 90% confidence, or high-risk action based on unverified evidence.
SILENCEConfidence below 50%, or incomplete evidence. The refusal is recorded.
WAITElevated risk or insufficient confidence for execution. Human review is required.
ACT NOWVerified evidence, acceptable risk, and confidence of at least 80%.

02 · Signed Proof Chain

Each block contains the question, answer, verdict, rule, and hash of the previous block. Any change becomes visible during verification.

REJECT · FAIL-CLOSED · CHAIN INTEGRITY LOST

Manipulation detected. Execution is blocked, the breach is preserved as evidence, and the case must be escalated to the named human operator.

Regulatory positioning. Proof-of-Silence™ supports key EU AI Act requirements including record-keeping, traceability and human oversight. It is an accountability layer, not a certification, and does not by itself make a system compliant.
